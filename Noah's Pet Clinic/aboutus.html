              Manchester's primary pet care specialists, since 1983.

    About us

We are a family-run business in Connolly, Greater Manchester, with 35 years' experience looking after small animals.

Although we specialise in the treatment of dogs, we accept all small animals as patients, including birds and exotic pets.

And, while we recommend other local veterinary practices for the treatment of large animals, we will never turn any animal away in an emergency.


    Our values

Care

We believe in treating all animals like royalty. Your pets are part of your family.
We will treat them as if they are part of ours.


Understanding

We understand the worry that pet owners feel towards their little friends. We aim to show you that we care just as much about them as you.


Trust

We know the trust you place in us when you leave your animals in our care. We will never act in anything but their best interests, at all times.
